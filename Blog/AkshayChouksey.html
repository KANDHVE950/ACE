Akshay Chouksey, a student of Civil Engineering from IIT-Guwahati secured AIR-1 in GATE 2018.


1141 is the population of Bilakta, the village where Akash Chouksey was born . Part of the district Seoni in Madhya Pradesh , the main occupation in Bilakta is farming. So how does someone from such a humble background achieve what so many people dream of.




Based on the impeccable results in secondary school examination, Akash was selected in a program and sent to Bhopal for the preparation of JEE Advanced. Achieving yet another success, he started his bachelor’s programme at Department of Civil Engineering, IIT Guwahati. The journey of securing AIR 1 in gate examination began with a crash course from ace academy Delhi . This was followed by 6 months of rigorous hard work along with continuous tests and revisions.

Being a determined person, he understood the significance of discipline in life. He regularly went to the library from 10 to 2 after which he studied for 2 more hours in his room . Covering 2 to 3 topics from 2 subjects everyday Akash used his time very efficiently . He used to make micro notes of all the topics he had covered and used to keep reading them whenever he had the time

For The Interview round,

Namit emphasized on the fact that the Detailed Application Form (DAF) which is given before the interview, should be filled very meticulously. It can be instrumental in turning the tide of the interview in the candidate’s favour. Namit kept connected with his friends from college who has previously cracked the exam. He states that it really helped him monitor his growth throughout his preparation.


In addition to the UPSC – ESE, Namit has also cleared various other exams such as GATE, BARC, PGCIL and IOCL.


He plans to join the railway sector after his training.
